About

Message transmission has been the key to communication since man started to read and write. Efficient tracking of the packets has become a key part of modern day work culture. We, at rShipper, bring to you a social platform where you can have a profile of your own and monitor the couriers sent/being received by the recipient.

From generating AWBs to tracking real-time packet location, we offer a multitude of options to explore and have a hassle-free service at your usage. Our vision is to provide a network of all types of courier services under one roof so the world can benefit by the ease of use of something as niche as their daily routine of work culture. India is home to 1.3 Billion people and we wish to empower them in a manner that regular vendors would never do.
